"The Quick and the Dead" (1995) - Character Guide

For your tournament app's "Character Alignment" feature, these are the iconic gunslingers from the film. Players can choose whose personality or style they most closely align with.

The Main Cast

Ellen ("The Lady")

Played by: Sharon Stone

Alignment Profile: The Driven Avenger. Mysterious, quiet, and haunted by her past. She doesn't fight for glory or money, but for a singular, deeply personal vendetta. She is methodical, incredibly fast, and carries the emotional weight of the tournament.

John Herod

Played by: Gene Hackman

Alignment Profile: The Ruthless Tycoon. The cruel, narcissistic ruler of the town of Redemption. He is wealthy, impeccably dressed, and rules through pure fear and intimidation. He is a master gunslinger who toys with his opponents and takes pleasure in breaking them psychologically before killing them.

Cort

Played by: Russell Crowe

Alignment Profile: The Reluctant Warrior. A former notorious outlaw who found religion and became a pacifist preacher. He is dragged into the tournament against his will by Herod. He refuses to wear a gun until forced, fighting an internal battle between his violent past and his peaceful vows.

Fee "The Kid"

Played by: Leonardo DiCaprio

Alignment Profile: The Cocky Prodigy. Young, brash, flashy, and desperate to prove himself. He runs a gun store and is incredibly fast, but his main motivation is winning the respect of Herod, whom he believes to be his father.

Notable Contestants & Townspeople

Ace Hanlon

Played by: Lance Henriksen

Alignment Profile: The Boastful Showman. An arrogant, flamboyant gunfighter who loves the sound of his own voice. He dresses like a dime-novel hero, performs flashy gun tricks, and fabricates legends about his own exploits to intimidate others.

Sergeant Cantrell

Played by: Keith David

Alignment Profile: The Hired Professional. A disciplined, highly skilled mercenary and former soldier. He is practical, calm under pressure, and is secretly hired by the townspeople to enter the tournament specifically to take down Herod.

Spotted Horse

Played by: Jonothon Gill

Alignment Profile: The Unbreakable Survivor. A fierce Native American fighter wrapped in chains and folklore. He enters the tournament claiming that he cannot be killed by a bullet, absorbing heavy damage and striking fear into his opponents through sheer resilience.

Scars

Played by: Mark Boone Junior

Alignment Profile: The Brutal Outlaw. A filthy, vicious, and unhinged escaped convict. He fights dirty, lacks any sense of honor, and relies on pure, rugged aggression.

Kelly

Played by: Tobin Bell

Alignment Profile: The Cold Calculator. A quiet, terrifyingly precise gunslinger. He doesn't show off; he simply stares down his opponents with dead eyes and lets his gun do the talking.

Gutzon

Played by: Sven-Ole Thorsen

Alignment Profile: The Intimidating Bruiser. A massive, towering Swedish champion. He relies on his imposing physical size and terrifying presence to win fights before the draw even happens.
